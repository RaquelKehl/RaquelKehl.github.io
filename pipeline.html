Pipeline.

A scrambled network, one core, many endpoints. Rotate the tiles until everything connects — in as few moves as possible. It’s the day job, with better feedback loops.

How to play
  • Goal: connect every tile to the copper core
  • Rotate: click / tap a tile, or arrows + enter
  • Score: fewest moves — par is the scramble’s minimum
  • Best scores are saved on this device only
Why this game

Every transformation is this puzzle: the pieces exist, the connections don’t. Fewer moves means fewer manual steps — the 60% on the homepage, played out on a grid.

Under the hood

Vanilla Canvas 2D, zero dependencies. Each level is a randomised spanning tree, scrambled by rotation — always solvable, with par computed as the true minimum number of rotations. Connectivity floods from the core each move; win detection, packet animation, and a reduced-motion fallback included.
