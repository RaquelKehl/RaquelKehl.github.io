Behind the scenes — a little context about me

The human in the system

Rising sun meets Andes skyline — Japanese structure, Latin dynamic — was my personal big bang, and everything since carries both: a Wirtschaftsinformatikerin (a hybrid of business administration and software engineering) who translates management jargon into byte-sized instructions for developers and board expectations into concrete, auditable systems. I fuse structural logic with strategic agility, moving comfortably from architecture diagrams and data flows to governance models and risk controls, backed by child-like enthusiasm, curiosity and energy, plus a dry, quietly geeky sense of humour. My work centres on the strategic use of technology and AI: I build and refine operating models where business goals, risk, accountability and everyday workflows are aligned — so organisations can move decisively instead of just reacting.
